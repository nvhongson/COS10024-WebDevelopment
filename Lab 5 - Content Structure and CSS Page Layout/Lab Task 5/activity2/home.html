Unit Delivery Survey

You are invited to partcipate in a research trial as a part of the teaching and learning research by Nathan. He is the Faculty of Science , Engineering and Technology of Swinburne University of Technology

The Research Project

This study aims to collect feedback from students undertaking Web Development. This is in order to evaluate the delivery of the unit. Results of the evaluation will be of interest to the lecturers to help them determine whether our approach is likely to enhance the learning outcomes of the students.
