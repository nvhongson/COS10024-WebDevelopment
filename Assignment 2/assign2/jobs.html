Position Title: Marketing Manager

Reference Number - MM101

Brief Description: The Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and implementing marketing strategies that align with the company's goals and objectives.

Salary Range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year

Reports to: Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop and implement comprehensive marketing strategies to increase brand awareness and drive sales
  2. Create and execute marketing campaigns across various channels, including email, social media, and advertising
  3. Analyze market trends and customer behavior to inform marketing strategies and campaigns
  4. Manage the company's website and social media presence, ensuring all content is up-to-date and engaging
  5. Collaborate with other departments to ensure marketing efforts align with overall company goals and objectives

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes

Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in marketing or related field
  • Minimum of 5 years' experience in marketing, with a proven track record of success
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities

Preferable

  • Master's degree in marketing or related field
  • Experience in the textile industry
  • Proficiency in digital marketing tools and techniques
  • Experience in managing a team

Position Title: Law Associate

Reference Number - LI101

Brief Description: A Law Associate objective is to help Ceylon's Law Division with all the copyright directed work and any legal & liability problems.'

Salary Range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year

Reports to: Company's Lawyer / Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Key Responsibilities

  1. Assist the company in the technical law department.
  2. Create and file patent for copyrights of products produced by the company.
  3. Defend the company along with the company's barrister when required.
  4. Manage any litigation the company might fall under.
  5. Represent the company and all personnel as long as requested by CEO.

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes

Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in law
  • Minimum of 5 years' experience in marketing, with a proven track record of success
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work within a team (both in leading and participating)

Preferable

  • At least 2-3 years of practice in a firm
  • Previous experience in the field
  • Knowledge about sales/privacy/copyright laws
  • Experience in managing a team

About Ceylon Textile Suppliers
